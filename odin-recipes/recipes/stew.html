Slow Cooker Beef Stew

Perfect for chilly evenings, and effortless to prepare, this slow cooked beef stew will never let you down.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef, sliced into 1 inch cubes
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ tspn salt
  • ½ tspn ground black pepper
  • 4 medium carrots, sliced
  • 3 medium potatoes, diced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 ½ cups beef broth
  • 1 tspn Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tspn ground paparika
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 large bay leaf

How to prepare

  1. Place beef into slow cooker.

  2. Mix flour, salt, and pepper in a small bowel and pour over beef. Stir until beef is fully coated.

  3. Add beef broth, carrots, potatoes, onion, celery, Worcestershire sauce, paprika, garlic, and the bay leaf; stif to combine.

    4. Cover, and cooker until beef is tender enough to cut with a spoon on Low for 8-12 hours, or on High for 4-6 hours

  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
