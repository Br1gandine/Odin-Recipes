Tandoori Chicken

Marinaded Tandoori Chicken Thighs

Classic indian tandoori chicken to impress yours friends on your next game night.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs
  • 1 medium lemon, juiced
  • 1 ¼ cups plain yoghurt
  • ½ medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 2 tspn garam masala
  • 1 tspn grated fresh ginger root
  • 1 tspn cayenne pepper
  • 1 tspn red food coloring (optional)
  • 1 tspn yellow food coloring (optional)
  • 2 tspn finely chopped cilantro
  • 1 medium lemon, cut into wedges

How to prepare

  1. Remove and discard skin from chicken. score the meat and place into a shallow dish. Season chicken with lemon juice and salt, ensuring that the meat is fully coated. Let sit for 20 mins

  2. Mix yogurt, onion, garlic, garam masala, ginger, and cayenne pepper together in a medium bowl until smooth, then stir in food coloring.

  3. Spread yoghurt mixture over chicken, cover and refrigerate for 6-24 hours (the longer the better).

  4. When ready to cook, preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate. Remove chicken from marinade. Discard remaining marinade.

  5. Cook chicken on the preheated grill until no longer pink and the juices run clear. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).

  6. Garnish with cilantro and lemon wedges, and serve on a bed of your favourite rice.
